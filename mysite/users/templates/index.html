Welcome To TASSA

TASSA is South Africa's web-based Student Application Centre.
An engine built to simplify the Ever-so time consuming South African College and University application process.
TASSA allows users to essentially Choose all their desired Courses (offered by the multiple different institutions in SA);
fill in ONE application form (for all) and finally;
let TASSA do the rest.

How To Guide

 
Step 1: Create Account Step 2: Log In Step 3: Course Research Step 4: Get Necessary Documents Step 5: Apply
 
Copy of ID (Certified) or Passport (Certified) Copy of Gr11/Gr12 results (Certified) Proof of Payment from Registered Bank to Acct: 12120987
 
The sum of the negotiated application fees of the Tertiary Institutions selected combined with R15 for each institution applied to through TASSA must be paid (and the proof of payment submitted with your application) in order for your One for Many application to be received, processed and distributed to your desired Colleges and Universities.
 
Responses from your Colleges and Universities can be seen in the Track section (after you've logged into your account) Responses will also be sent directly to your Email Address You will be allowed to make amendments to your applications and submit extra required documents from the APPLY section No extra fees will be charged for amendments to - or extra document resubmissions for - applications already paid for and submitted through TASSA.
